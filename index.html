Hi, I'm Xuefei Yan

I’m currently a first-year Ph.D. student in the School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University, under the supervision of Dr. Cheng-Jun Wang. I am also a member of Computational Communication Collaboratory and the Socrates Lab. I am interested in computational social science, time use research, computational narrative and causal inference.  My studies have been published in Chinese Journal of Communication.
